Rules of MahJong Solitaire

  • The goal of the game is to clear all 144 tiles from the board.
  • A tile can only be cleared from the board by matching it with another identical tile.
  • A tile is eligible to be matched if it has no tile on its left border or right border or another tile on top of it.
  • Once a tile is matched with another identical tile, both are removed from the board and the player is able to make additional matches
  • A player can undo moves with no penalty.
  • When all tiles have been cleared, the game has been won!
